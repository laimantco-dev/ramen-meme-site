Recipe of the Day: Degen Upgraded Ramen

Turn basic instant ramen into a flavor bomb. Perfect for late-night trading sessions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack instant ramen (seasoning packet discarded)
  • 1/2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • Handful sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup water
  • Handful fresh spinach
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 green onion (sliced)
  • Sriracha or chili oil to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sautÃ© garlic, ginger, and mushrooms until soft (\~2 min).
  2. Add broth + water, bring to a boil.
  3. Add noodles, cook \~3 min until tender.
  4. Stir in spinach until wilted.
  5. Crack egg directly into the broth, poach 3 min (yolk runny for max degen energy).
  6. Serve in bowl, top with green onion + sriracha. Slurp & HODL!
